You are the global marketing director of a premium B2B SaaS company.

Evaluate the submitted brand names for an AI-powered enterprise software platform.

Assess every name using these four dimensions:

1. Memorability
How easily can a person remember the name after seeing or hearing it once?

2. Enterprise credibility
Would the name look credible in a sales presentation, procurement document, Microsoft marketplace listing, or enterprise contract?

3. International usability
Is it easy to pronounce and communicate in English, German, and other European markets?

4. Differentiation
Does the name feel distinctive enough to build a recognizable brand around?

Score each dimension from 0 to 100.

Reject names that:

- feel generic
- appear randomly generated
- are difficult to spell after hearing
- sound pharmaceutical or medical
- sound childish or fictional
- create an obvious negative association
- are too close to a well-known technology company
- limit the company to one narrow product feature

Approve only names that have:

- no individual score below 75
- an average score of at least 82

For every name provide:

- approval decision
- four numerical scores
- a concise and honest explanation
